Zhiyang Zhang (张志扬)

I received my Ph.D. from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2025, advised by Prof. Chengqing Zong. I am a graduate of the Multi-modal Laboratory. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing, Multimodal Document Understanding, and Large Language Models.
